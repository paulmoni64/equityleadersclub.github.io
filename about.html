DeKUTEQUITY LEADERS CLUB

EQUITY LEADERS PROGRAM-DeKUT CHAPTER

Equity Leaders’ Program is a club that brings together students that have been beneficiaries of
Equity Bank either as EALPs (Equity African Leaders Program) or WTF (Wings to Fly) alumni.
The EALP members are those that earned an internship to work in the bank branches situated in
various distinct parts of the country immediately after finishing their O levels. WTF alumni
members, who can also be members of EALP so long as they got to work in the bank branches,
are members that got sponsored by Equity Bank through Equity Group Foundation in their
secondary schooling but unfortunately failed to earn internships to work with the bank.

ELP, or rather ELC as it is famously referred to, has members in all universities in the country and
each university identified as a chapter. For instance, Dedan Kimathi University of Technology is
called DeKUT Chapter. Members in these chapters are basically students pursuing their respective
courses at the universities they are in.

ELP-DeKUT chapter boasts of 112 active members some of which are EALP members and others
WTF alumni. To ensure excellent cohesiveness and maximum self-development, this chapter is
involved in activities that not only allows its members to bond like a family but only grow and
develop to be dependable leaders at a personal level. Our activities range from community
involvement such as visiting Children’s homes and empathizing with the children as a way of
giving back to the immediate society to academic mentorship to WTF scholars in secondary
schools within the Central Region. The chapter also organizes professional training annually so as
to train its members and equip them with requisite skills in job seeking and portraying themselves
as potential employees.

The chapter is being led by elected leaders whom the members are so much proud of. The leaders
provide the zeal and zest that keeps the progress of the chapter afloat and are actually the
brainchildren of most of the activities undertaken by the chapter. In accordance with the
constitution of ELC, DeKUT chapter is governed by 5 leaders and Year Representatives. They
include:

  1. Chapter Leader
  2. Deputy Chapter Leader
  3. Organizing Secretary
  4. Treasurer
  5. Mentorship Coordinator
  6. Year Representatives (Year 1,2,3 and 4)

This chapter cherishes and respects the positive values, beliefs and cultural practices that its
members are involved in and is, at any given time, not discriminatory against anyone on the
mentioned subjects. We dearly respect our diversity as members since it is our strength.
Furthermore, we share common virtues as which include the following: teamwork, commitment,
integrity, hard work among others. We all live to see the fulfillment of the chapter’s
objectives to an epic degree!
